Who We Are

Built for this specific situation.

MDC Tax Services was built around a single, underserved need: US citizens living in France who require expert, ongoing guidance navigating two of the world's most complex tax systems.

Our Mission

Not transactional.
Relational.

Most tax preparers treat each filing as a standalone event. You explain your situation, they file, and you're on your own until next year. That model doesn't work for people living across borders.

Cross-border taxation is cumulative. Decisions you make this year affect what's possible next year. An investment account opened without guidance becomes a compliance headache. A property sale handled incorrectly triggers amendments and penalties.

We built MDC Tax Services to be the firm that stays with you—that knows your situation deeply, tracks the changes that matter, and plans ahead so you're never scrambling.

"We anticipate the future and take the whole picture into account—setting you up for this year and the ones to come."
