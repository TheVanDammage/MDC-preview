What We Do

Comprehensive cross-border tax services

We work exclusively with individuals—not businesses—and focus on building lasting relationships that grow with your financial life.

US & French Tax Preparation

Annual filing for both countries, handled together. We ensure your US obligations—including FBAR—are met every year, and get you compliant if you've fallen behind.

  • FBAR (Foreign Bank Account Report) compliance
  • Annual individual tax filing for both countries
  • Catch-up filing and compliance programs

Cross-Border Tax Advice

Before you open an account, sell a property, or make an investment—understand the implications on both sides of the Atlantic. We give you the full picture upfront.

  • PEA, Assurance Vie & foreign brokerage consequences
  • Property sales and rental income reporting
  • Investment and capital gains implications

Estates & Successions

Cross-border inheritance and estate matters require specialized knowledge. We handle the filings, flag the obligations, and help you navigate what can be a complex process.

  • Cross-border inheritance filings
  • Foreign estate compliance
  • Estate tax reporting obligations
